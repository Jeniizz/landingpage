Our Vissions

With the purpose of providing a more convenient and comfortable lifestyle, encouraging people to focus more about their lives and connecting with the community, we’d like to open a coffee shop near the schools in Cergy. The coffee shop will mainly serve coffee, drinks and desserts as well as high-nutrition food at a fair price for students and employees during lunch time. In the afternoons, an atelier will be opened to teach customers who are interested in the process of making coffee, to do latte arts. At the same time, provide a “third space” environment for working and studying. In the evenings, the shop will be transitioned into a bistro with on-site live band, and the lightings will be adjusted with a centrol-controlled dimmer, aiming to provide a comfortable and chill space for people to relax and socialise after work.
